MGD EDITING INTERFACE: Design and Requirements - Marker Non-Mouse


Introduction

Non-mouse markers, necessary for the curation of mouse homology data, are stored in the database with limited information.

Downloads from Species-specific databases are used to update the Symbol attributes for non-mouse species on a regular basis (MGI Homology Loads).

Non-mouse markers can also be modified directly from the Homology editing form. However, a new feature to allow the association of Sequence Accession numbers to non-mouse markers has prompted the addition of a separate editing form for the curation of non-mouse markers. This editing form is launched from the Homology editing form's Edit->Marker Information menu selection.

MGD EDITING INTERFACE: Design and Requirements - Crosses


Introduction

The master list of Crosses contains the definitions for all Crosses used in all Cross Experiments. Some of these Crosses are well-defined, such as the Copeland-Jenkins crosses, and are re-used over and over again by many Experiments. Some Crosses are one-of-a-kind, anonymous crosses which are used only once by one Experiment.

The well-defined Crosses have a non-null Cross name and may be designated for display in the Cross Listing lookup list.
